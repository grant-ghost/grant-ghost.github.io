Who we are

My name is Brittany, and I am a Technical Writer for the Faculty of Mathematics at the University of Waterloo. Since November 2018, I have helped to secure over $5M CAD in funding for Waterloo researchers. I previously managed the Machine Learning Research Group at the University of Guelph, where I helped to secure over $990,000 CAD in funding over two years.

What we do

With experience writing grants and editing research papers, we are the solution for your writing needs.

Grants from Scratch

Not sure where to start? We can meet with you and establish project goals and timelines, and get you well on your way to submitting your grant proposal.

Grant Editing

Have you already written a grant proposal, but not sure that you're on the right track? We can provide feedback and editing so that you can be confident in your proposal submission.

Project Planning

Not sure if your project plan will look good on a grant proposal? We can help you to establish realistic timelines, milestones, and budgets that align with your desired funding program.

Document Review

Preparing big documents that are not grant proposals, but still need help? We can do that. With experience editing documents ranging from academic papers to media releases and web content, we can give your work a final polish.
